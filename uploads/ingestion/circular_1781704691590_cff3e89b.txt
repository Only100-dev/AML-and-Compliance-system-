CBUAE Notice 3551/2021
Title: Anti-Money Laundering and Combating the Financing of Terrorism Guidelines for Financial Institutions
Issuing Authority: Central Bank of the United Arab Emirates
Publication Date: 2021-12-15
Effective Date: 2022-01-01

Summary: This notice establishes comprehensive AML/CFT/CPF requirements for all financial institutions operating under CBUAE supervision, including insurance companies. It mandates risk-based customer due diligence, suspicious activity reporting, and sanctions screening obligations.

Key Obligations:
1. IT Department must implement real-time transaction monitoring systems within 90 days.
2. Compliance Department must designate a Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO) within 30 days.
3. Operations Department must establish customer risk rating methodologies within 60 days.
4. Front Office must conduct enhanced due diligence for all high-risk customers.
5. Finance Department must allocate budget for AML compliance tools annually.

Risk Impact: HIGH
Affected Departments: IT, Compliance, Operations, Front Office, Finance
